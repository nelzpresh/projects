Kale caesar quinoa salad with roasted chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 middle.

    2. Time: 4 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated

    3. Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add wainuts and olive oil as garnish

    4. Time: 1 minute
